The Many Peoples of Sultan Kudarat

Ethnic Diversity of Sultan Kudarat

Hiligaynon

The majority group, settled in the 17th–18th centuries, influencing language, festivals, and daily life.

Maguindanaon

Majority of the Muslim community, contributing rich cultural traditions, music, and dances.

Manobo, Teduray, Blaan

Indigenous groups with unique traditions, clothing, and rituals that enrich Sultan Kudarat's cultural tapestry.

Ilocanos and Cebuanos

Newer settlers adding diversity to local customs, food, and language.
