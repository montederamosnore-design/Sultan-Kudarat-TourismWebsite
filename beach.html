Enjoy the Coastal Beauty of Sultan Kudarat

Relax by the Sea

Although much of Sultan Kudarat is mountainous, it also features coastal areas like Kalamansig, where visitors can enjoy beach activities and marine life.

What to Do

  • Swimming and sunbathing
  • Exploring coastal villages
  • Enjoying fresh seafood

The area is also known for its rich marine biodiversity, making it an important ecological zone.
