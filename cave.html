Cave Exploration

Discover Underground Wonders

Adventure Beneath the Surface

Cave exploration in Sultan Kudarat offers a thrilling experience for adventurers. Many caves in the province remain untouched, showcasing natural rock formations formed over thousands of years.

Tenubak Cave

One of the known cave systems is found near Tenubak Falls in Lebak. Inside, visitors can see massive stalactites and stalagmites, along with underground rivers flowing through the cave.

Experience

  • Exploring dark and wide cave chambers
  • Crossing streams and underground rivers
  • Seeing unique rock formations
Visitors are advised to bring proper gear and guides for safety during exploration.
