Tres Andanas Falls

A Three-Layered Natural Wonder

Introduction

Tres Andanas Falls is one of the most famous natural attractions in Sultan Kudarat, located in the municipality of Lebak. Its name comes from its unique three-tiered structure.

Natural Beauty

The waterfall is surrounded by thick forest, towering trees, and cool mountain air. The sound of rushing water combined with the peaceful environment makes it a perfect place to relax.

Experience

  • Swim in clean and refreshing water pools
  • Take photos of the cascading waterfalls
  • Explore nearby forest trails

Tips for Visitors

Wear comfortable footwear  |  Bring extra clothes  |  Respect nature
