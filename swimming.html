Swimming at Waterfalls

A Refreshing Escape in Sultan Kudarat

Nature's Hidden Pools

Sultan Kudarat is home to several breathtaking waterfalls that offer a refreshing retreat from the heat, surrounded by lush forests.

Tres Andanas Falls

Located in Lebak, Tres Andanas Falls is known for its unique three-layered cascade. The cool, clear water flows down naturally formed rock steps, creating inviting pools where visitors can swim and unwind.

What to Expect

  • Cold and refreshing natural pools
  • Scenic surroundings with thick vegetation
  • Peaceful atmosphere away from the city

Reaching these waterfalls often involves trekking through forest trails, adding excitement to the experience.
