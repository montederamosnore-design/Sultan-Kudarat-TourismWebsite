Tenubak Cave

An Underground Adventure

Introduction

Tenubak Cave is part of a natural attraction located in Lebak, Sultan Kudarat. It is often visited together with Tenubak Falls, making it a complete adventure destination.

Inside the Cave

The cave features impressive rock formations such as stalactites hanging from the ceiling and stalagmites rising from the ground, developed over thousands of years.

Unique Experience

  • Walking through dark cave passages
  • Exploring underground streams
  • Discovering unique rock formations
Visitors are encouraged to bring flashlights, proper footwear, and a guide to ensure safety.
