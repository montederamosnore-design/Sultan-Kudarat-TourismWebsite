People and Culture of Sultan Kudarat

Demographics

According to the 2020 census, the province of Sultan Kudarat has a population of approximately 854,052 people, with a population density of about 160 people per square kilometer.

Population Growth

The population expanded rapidly especially between the 1970s and 2000s as more settlers from different parts of the Philippines moved into the province.

Ethnic Groups and Languages

The majority are Hiligaynons. Other groups include Maguindanaon, Cebuano, Ilocano, Karay-a, Blaan, Teduray, and Manobo communities.

Religion

The largest religious group is Roman Catholic, followed by Islam. Others include Iglesia ni Cristo, Seventh-day Adventists, and various Protestant denominations.

Quick Facts:

  • Population (2020): 854,052
  • Density: 160 people/km²
  • Main Ethnic Group: Hiligaynon
  • Major Religions: Catholicism and Islam
  • Main Languages: Hiligaynon, Cebuano, Maguindanaon

Economy

The economy is mainly based on agriculture. Major crops include rice, corn, coconut, coffee, bananas, and mangoes. Fishing is also important in coastal areas.
